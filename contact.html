Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply to KOSEN-KMITL?

Applications are typically open during October-December each year. Visit our admissions page or contact the admissions office for detailed requirements. You will need to submit academic transcripts, recommendation letters, and pass an entrance examination.

What are the tuition fees?

Tuition fees vary by program. Please contact the admissions office for the most current fee structure. Scholarships are available for qualified students, including merit-based and need-based financial aid.

Is there dormitory accommodation?

Yes, KMITL provides on-campus dormitory facilities for students. Contact Student Affairs for availability and booking information. Priority is given to first-year and international students.

Do I need to know Japanese before applying?

No prior Japanese knowledge is required. Japanese language courses are integrated into the curriculum starting from Year 1. However, students are expected to achieve JLPT N3 level by graduation.

Important: All prospective students must meet the English proficiency requirements. Check our admissions page for specific requirements.

About the Program

The KOSEN-KMITL program is an innovative 5-year educational program that combines the best of Japanese KOSEN (National Institute of Technology) education system with Thai engineering excellence at KMITL.

What is KOSEN?

KOSEN (高等専門学校 - Kōtō Senmon Gakkō) is Japan's unique engineering education system that accepts students after junior high school and provides 5 years of integrated education focusing on practical engineering skills.

Program Highlights

  • Practical Learning: Hands-on experience from Year 1
  • Industry Connection: Direct partnerships with leading companies
  • Bilingual Education: Instruction in both English and Japanese
  • International Exposure: Exchange programs with Japanese KOSEN schools
  • Early Career Start: Graduate as skilled engineers in 5 years
